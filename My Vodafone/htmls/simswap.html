Got a blank SIM? Moving your phone number to a blank SIM card is super easy!

  •   Getting started

    • 1. Before you start please back up your contacts

    2. Keep your My Vodafone password handy.

    3. Have a blank Vodafone SIM card with you

    4. Ensure the ID number provided matches the ID used for registration

    Once successful , the process can take up to 2 hrs to complete.
